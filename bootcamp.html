About Bootcamp/Ideas Project Development Camp

NIDA hope to annually use this camp to inculcate and introduce the concept of thinking creatively and critically when making choices in life. Both methods of logic and empirical reasoning will be introduced with simple lectures and fun activities. Also competitive and collaborative methods of producing an outcome will be explored by the participants.

Our Target Group

Our target group for CCTHB/IPDC will have 3 different Levels of Camp target groups.

  • Primary
  • Secondary School
  • University Students
  • Graduate for project camp

Conclusion

NIDA believes that this program can be a platform for the young generation of students in Nigeria to develop their creative and critical thinking capabilities which will help them be an effective persons within their own community and the nation.
