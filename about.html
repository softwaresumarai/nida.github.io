Awards

The nigerian inventors development academy is a non-profit organization that is positioned to corporate with other nigerian higher institutions, governmental and non-profit research institutes, hubs, individual inventor, and research fellows that will span from our institutions and communities nationwide.

This platform enhances the visibility of institutions of technology and non-profit research institutions of technology development, promote entrepreneurship and become advocates for inspiring academic inventions/innovation in our various institutions of learning & communities.

  • An important factor of NIDA is to recognize and encourage inventors with patent issued from Nigerian patent and trademark office and those without patent but registered business names to enhance the visibility of institutions technology and innovation research.
  • This will pioneer the establishment IP portfolios in institutions in Nigeria and to also promote the disclosure of intellectual property for revenue generation.
  • NIDA will train, educate and mentor nigerians including students to translate their project works to prototypes which will in turn becomes valuable finished products that will attract investment for the creation of industries for jobs, generate revenue for the benefit of nations economic sustainability growth.

The NIDA Advisory Board consists of Top Exemplary experts within strategically chosen innovations ecosystems.These leaders in the ecosystems will be available to appear at invention and innovtion shows & events, educational programs, summit and expo etc. as well as assisting the NIDA Executive team in achieving the NIDA goals to become the undisputable leader in the education, Innovation, and Invention Industry. Providing guidance, Knowledge, Education and Resources to protect the higher institutions Inventors and researchers is a main stay of the NIDA.
